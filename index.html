📌 Workforce Planning

Identify the need, define the job role, and set a timeline. This is where strategic hiring begins.

🔍 Sourcing Candidates

Post on job portals, LinkedIn, or engage agencies. Build a diverse pipeline of potential hires.

Analyse resumes, conduct initial calls, and filter the most suitable candidates.

🗣️ Interview Process

Schedule interviews and assess the candidate’s skills, attitude, and team compatibility.

📄 Selection & Offer

Finalize the best fit, share offer letters, and handle salary negotiations professionally.

🚀 Onboarding

Welcome the new hire, complete paperwork, and initiate training for a great start.
